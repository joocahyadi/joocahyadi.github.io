Collaboration

Analysis of the Indonesian Economy and Recommendations for Placement of UMKMs based on Geolocation and Social Media Sentiments in Jakarta



Author: Nelson Alexander Owen, Steven Alexander Liong, Joshia Cahyadi, Hansel Noble Lawrence



Introduction:


The emergence of COVID-19 pandemic in early 2020, caused the Indonesian economy hit hard. With the pandemic lasting almost 2 years, it is necessary to know the current condition of the Indonesian economy in order to know the progress of Indonesia. In this project, we analyzed one of the main keys to the Indonesian economy, namely UMKM. We also keen to see the distribution of mapping and public opinion about UMKM in Jakarta. In the end, we provided conclusions and suggestions to the public and authorities regarding UMKM in Jakarta.

NFT Creator Recommendations and Dashboard



Author: Steven Alexander Liong, Joshia Cahyadi

Mentored by: Louis Owen



Introduction:


Non-Fungible Token (NFT), one of the biggest phenomena in late 2021, has stormed the digital marketplace. The NFT’s phenomenon influenced various societies, especially artists and influencers. In addition to them, many organizations also started to create their original NFT projects and collections for many purposes, such as engaging their fans in this online era. Unfortunately, not everyone who started NFT projects has succeeded. Most of them failed to maintain and continue their NFT projects.


In this project, we analyzed many aspects from the 3 given datasets using predefined assumptions and gained insights related to the NFTs distributed in Opensea. We also did creator segmentation to give suggestions for beginner creators that wanted to start their journey in the NFT world.
