Collaboration

Analysis of the Indonesian Economy and Recommendations for Placement of UMKMs based on Geolocation and Social Media Sentiments in Jakarta



Author: Nelson Alexander Owen, Steven Alexander Liong, Joshia Cahyadi, Hansel Noble Lawrence



Introduction:


The emergence of COVID-19 pandemic in early 2020, caused the Indonesian economy hit hard. With the pandemic lasting almost 2 years, it is necessary to know the current condition of the Indonesian economy in order to know the progress of Indonesia. In this project, we analyzed one of the main keys to the Indonesian economy, namely UMKM. We also keen to see the distribution of mapping and public opinion about UMKM in Jakarta. In the end, we provided conclusions and suggestions to the public and authorities regarding UMKM in Jakarta.
